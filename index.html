ORIGIN STORY

In the beginning there was nothing but a God by the name of Anant. One day Anant created the universe with an explosion of energy from his chest. This force created 11 galaxies and a combined total of 5,000 unique Gods.

All the Gods were wired in their own unique way, yet they all had one thing in common -- They were all made from the same creative energy.

Will they be able to work together and collaborate to build the new universe? We will find out!

MISSION STATEMENT

We believe everyone is a creator. Some love mastering their artistic craft, while others don’t identify as an ‘artist’, yet still have a deep desire to use their imagination & create. That was us.

This desire led us to hire 80 illustrators full time that could help bring any of our, or your ideas, to life.

The Gods were made to give all community members access to be THE CREATOR. TO BE THE GOD.

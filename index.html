In the beginning...

In the beginning there was nothing but a God by the name of Anant. As the story goes, one day Anant created the universe by an explosion of energy from his chest. This force was said to be the strongest creative force in existence, creating 11 galaxies and a combined total of 10,000 unique Gods, spread across 3 races; Male, Female & Alien.

The 10k Gods were all wired in their own unique way, each with their own strengths & weaknesses, interests, & desires -- yet they all had one thing in common...They were all made of the same energy. They were all wired for creation at the deepest parts of their being. Whether they consciously knew it or not, they were all destined to create a new universe, just as Anant had with them.

Will they be able to work together and collaborate to create the new universe? Will they be able to get along, despite being different races, with different strengths & weaknesses, to achieve their common goal of living up to their potential of being an ultimate creative force in the universe? We will find out!
